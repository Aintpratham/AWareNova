How fast do I get access after paying?

Your license key is emailed automatically right after checkout, usually within minutes. Setup guidance is included, so most customers are up and running the same day.

Nova continuously watches for new Amazon warehouse opportunities and reacts the moment they appear, so you can respond to openings before most applicants notice them.

Yes — Nova runs on both Windows and macOS with the same workflow and speed, and no extra configuration.

Not at all. Setup is a short, guided process that takes a few minutes, and direct support is there if anything is unclear.

Nova removes the slowest part of the process — noticing that an opening exists and reacting to it. Which shifts appear still depends on your location and timing, so results vary from person to person.
